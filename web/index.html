⚾ MLB Pitch Predictor

Built with Python, Flask, scikit-learn, XGBoost, Pandas, pybaseball, and JavaScript.

Enter a game scenario (score, count, inning, runners, pitcher, etc.) and click Predict Pitch to see the most likely pitch type and average speed.

Trained on 2024 Statcast data. Results reflect model probabilities, not actual pitch calls. For demo purposes only.
